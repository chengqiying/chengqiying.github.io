Vision

User experience design in digital context
In this new era of industrial design, I've seen the design focus is transferring from product itself to user’s experience. Especially for digital product, form, emotional interaction and function details, are three vital factors which could greatly influence the life span of the product from user experience perspective. Think big and also think small details which live inside users’ pockets.

My vision is designing for user experience in digital context. I am passionate to explore how digital designs could well meet users’ needs and create thoughtful experience in the mean time. I hope users could be moved by the pleasant experience while using a digital product.

Identity

Beliefs in digital products to settle realistic problems
The new gained experience of designing digital interactive products triggers my interest. I have experienced the pleasure and convenience that digital products have brought to our life and I also see their potentials in the market. Thus, I would continue the journey of exploration of digital products as a UX designer.

Go for aesthetics and details
During the projects, I want all the works look beautiful, clean and simple. I care about details both in life and design. By observing details of people’s behaviours and questioning details of told stories, I have got the ability to empathise users by getting myself immersed into the specific context.

Goals

Develop my skills in interaction design of digital products
I don’t have the experience to design an application in well organized process before, so I expect to be capable of structuring a digital product design process through ideation, information architecture, wire frames, low-fidelity prototype and high fidelity prototype.



Practice and further develop the skill of user evaluation
The evaluation session in previous projects acted as the last step and the feedback has been seldom further leveraged to the next version of design. So, it is one of my goal to effectively conducting user evaluation sessions with different concepts, and combining massive test feedback and translate into one final design.



Develop prototyping skill of on-screen products
With some basic knowledge of HTML and CSS, I plan to learn prototyping out the design concept by using web application development technologies, such as JavaScript and Jquery Mobile, in order to let the users know about the design by experiencing it themselves.

Project

Objective
Based on all the feedback on the three directions in last semester, I decided to continute with the third direction which contains a social component. As mentioned both by Philips and my coach, the third direction appears to be the most interesting one and it has the potential to add possible value to the exsiting social network.

People could record, communicate and check their sleep data in this app. Through evaluating with users, I have the analysis of the usuability and experience of this application, and also users’ opinions about the social platform applied in sleep monitoring application.

What I have learned

Various digital product prototyping methods
In the early stage, I learned to use Quartz Composer to quickly develop rough ideas into interactive and communicatable prototypes. It is really helpful for discussing the ideas and collecting feedback. After having a complete concept, I explored an online rapid prototype tool -- Invision to have an overview of the whole interaction flow. For the final prototype, I learned the knowledge of web application prototyping such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript, in order to code out an interactive and usable prototype. After mastering all those prototyping methods, I could flexibly apply them in different iterations of design process according to the goals.

Developing usability and experience evaluation skill
This is the first time that I conducted usability and experience test in a standard process. After two rounds of the evaluation, I found usability test needs careful observation and recording, and experience requires an open attitude to user's opinions on the design. Through the evaluation session, I felt it is very essential to look at the design from a new user's perspective to gather fresh feedback as I have been quite familiar with it as a designer.

 

A systematical process of application design
In this semester, the first focus is to develop the chosen design direction into a presentable prototype. As mentioned in goals, I have realized the importance of a systematical process for application design. Before starting, I spent some efforts in learning how a professional team proceeds the design process in companies like Facebook. For application design, the first task is to clarify the information structure, which is quite essential because the following steps will rely on that. Wireframe will be created based on information structure, followed by interaction design which specifies how user could interaction with the app in related scenarios, and interface design which decides the appearance. The last step is to implement the static concept into an interactive prototype. With this structured process, a rough idea could be better developed into a sound concept. Also, the final design would be more convincing and clear.

This is a redesign task from Yelp during my job application. It is about to improve the mobile search flow of Yelp's app in five hours. The goal is to design an easy to use search section on Yelp's mobile application. I started this design exercise with a user scenario to empathize the experience of the current search flow in yelp. By analyzing the info structure of search section, I found three aspects which could be redesigned to improve the user experience. Through this design exercise, I have practiced my interaction design skill within a limited time. It is a good chance to challenge myself to design from a company perspective.
