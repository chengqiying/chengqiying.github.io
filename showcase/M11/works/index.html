Introduction

The client of this project is ING (International Netherland Group). ING’s mission is to set the standard in helping its customers manage their financial future. ING aims to deliver its financial products and services in the way its customers want them delivered: with exemplary service, convenience and at competitive prices.

Aligned with the needs of customers and the client (ING), this project designs a social media feedback system for ING. The goal of this project is to give customers the impression that their feedback is valued and appreciated by the bank, meanwhile help the bank make efficient use of the feedback from customers. This projects works on a social media platform, and one of the most popular social media, Facebook, is a trial in the project for this semester.

Activities

Service design methods

Have experienced this project, I learnt that it is vital for service designer to pick an apropriate tool to present the design concept as service design is an interdiscinplinary approach which combines different methods and tools from various discinplines. Indeed, I have grasped a few tools throughout this semester. Storyboard played an improtant role in the process of the project. I used it to ananlyze the current situation, prototyped my concepts and make comparison between them. which helped to communicate with users, expertise and client, and get feedback from them. Apart from that, Animation , video and Prezi are also the important tools in this project. Animation clearly shows the dynamic process of feedback changing. Prezi and video demonstrate the holistic concept.

Integrate different stakeholders

Various stakeholders are involved in a certain service. The ING users, ING and its staff and the social media platform are the main stakeholders in this project. I fousced more on the ING users and ING, and at the same time took the other stakeholders into considration. I can see how it helped me to get insights and inspiration in different iterations of the design. The new solutions came out from integrating the customer's and client's actual needs. I realized in my project that sometimes it would not be easy for deaigners to well combine the needs from both sides, I learnt to put the related stakeholders together on one map, and keep the bid picture in mind throughout every step.

Related literatures

At he beginning of the project, I spent some time reading the book "This Is Service Design". With this book, I got the first understanding of what is service design and realized that it is a burgeoning interdicsipline. In order to get inspirations,I searched the related articles online to study how the service design company (e.g. Engine)conduct a project. I would come to this book when confused about the methods and ways to deliver service design concept. I learnt the approaches of using stakeholder maps and storyboard from it. As for the storyboard, I gained deep and detailed skills to present the concept with storyboard effectively from the book:"Sketching User Experiences".

Reflection

I took this project as a start of learning service design. With the support of my coach, I have a better understanding of service design by the exploration in this project. As far as I am concerned now, service design is about efficiently planning and organizing all the elements in a certain service, in order to improve user experience and service quality. There are two vital ponits, one is user experience centered in every step of design. The other one is to bring the design into practice. Morevoer, I have grasped a course of service design tools which helped gain insights and feedback in the process of service design.

 

Reviewing the process from Ideation to the final results, I am very exciting that I have achieved th goals set for service design at the start. I have developed my competencies of User Focus and Perspective, Form and Senses, Idea and Concept and Business Process Design. As the communication with the cilent was not enough, which resulted in the weak improvement in Business Design in the project. However I gianed extra experience of it in the module DB514, so that I could applied it in future learning activities.
