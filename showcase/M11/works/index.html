Introduction

The client of this project is ING (International Netherland Group). ING’s mission is to set the standard in helping our customers manage their financial future. ING aims to deliver its financial products and services in the way its customers want them delivered: with exemplary service, convenience and at competitive prices.

Aligned with the needs of customers and the client (ING), this project designs a social media feedback system for ING. The goal of this project is to make customers feel that their feedback is valued and appreciated by the bank, and meanwhile help the bank make efficient use of the feedback from customers. This projects works on a social media platform, and one of the most popular social media, Facebook is a trial in the project for this semester.

Activities

Service design methods

Have experienced this project, I learned that it is vital for service designer to pick an apropriate tool to present the design concept as service design is an interdiscinplinary approach which combines different methods and tools from various discinplines. Indeed, I have learned a few tools throughout this semester. Storyboard played an improtant role in the process of the project. I used it to ananlyzed the current situation, prototyped my concepts and made comparison between them. which helped to communicate with users, expertise and client and get feedback from them. Apart from that, Animation , video and Prezi are also the important tools in this project. Animation clearly shows the dynamic process of feedback changing. Prezi and video demonstrate the holistic concept.

Integrate different stakeholders

Various stakeholders are involved in a certain service. The ING users, ING and its staff and the social media platform are the main stakeholders in this project. I fousced more on the ING users and ING, and at the same time took the other stakeholders into considration. I can see how it helped me to get insights and inspiration in different interations of the design. The new solutions came out from integrating the customer and client's actual needs. I learned to put the related stakeholders together on one map, and keep the bid picture in mind throughout every step.
