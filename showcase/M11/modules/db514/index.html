Module Introduction

It is a one week module which targets on Designing Business Processes, Design and Research Processes, Self-directed and Continuous Learning. We learn to design in an given context and go beyond the context where radical innovation opportunities can be created, also learn the skills to express and communicate the innovation opportunities to stakeholders.

With the expections of getting familiar with business processe and how to apply it in the design, and how to appropriately communicate the design with client, I choose this module as a chance to learn.  Starting the project in a team of six, by overcoming various setbacks together, we successfully finished the task of design products targeting on elderly people for Vogel's.

Activities

Concept

We strarted with a few quick brainstorms and presented the general idea in the midterm of this week. With the feedback and advice gotten from Vogel's and expertise, we rethink about the concept and changed it  to a mechanical systems that improve the ergonomics of the kitchen, and enhance the accessibility of cooking utensils and appliances in new kitchens, which can quickly but smoothly lifts the entire content of the drawer into John’s reach.

Communication with client

Cooperated with the client, Vogel's, I took this as a chance to learn to communicate with real client as I have little exeperience before. In the process, I find that a convincing way to express the innovaiton opportunity to a client is to bring out enough evidence and the practical design which aligned with their needs and profit.

Video expression

In process of this module, we presented the  finding and ideas all by videos. The first one tended to show the insights we obtained from field research. The rest two were designed to domenstrate the general and final concepts for Vogel's. I realized that video is  a good choice to show the idea to the client as a vivid way, which could not only give a deep impression, but also provideds the visual material to communicate with both client and expertise. From the persective of designers, it really improved the skill of  integrating the elements together to express the complete design.

Target user research

This time the group we decided to design for was active older adults who will increasingly need support as they get older. I learnt to experience the elderly's feelings by drawing the  one day in life map, imagining that what the life would be like when I was 65 years old. It is a new way to slip into the user's shoes to dig out the needs, which is different from the previous which only analyzes the current elderly people's life. Treat the elderly people as a group who also want to look sexy and fashionable.

Reflection

Design business process

I really appreciated that I could have an experience with a real client in the design process, which is lacked in my previous study. In the process, the assignments of this module and feedback from client made me seriously consider the business process of a designed product or service. I learned that for a real company, the only innovation is far from enough. How to incorporate with different stakeholders is crucial for the success of the product or service. Also, the financial issues needed to be taken into account in the design process, because that is a key aspect for a company to decide whether the new design is valuable to invest. This would be important for me to apply into continued study.

User focus and perspective

Sensing from different perspectives is very important, especially the user’s. When considering the user, I learned to use the tool “a day in life” to analyze users’ experience and find out the design point. When considering the market, I learned to take the business model canvas to think about the ways these stakeholders were incorporated. In this module, our target group was the elderly people. It seemed that the normal way to understand the elderly was to interview them. However, I found that another good way was to imagine that when I was a 70 years old woman, what my life would be like. This kind of way to experience the user could bring the user insights which were easily to be neglected.

Teamwork and communication

It was my first time to be engaged in a group of six students here in TU/e, five of which are Dutch. And at the beginning, it was indeed a challenge for me to integrate into them. After thinking about the different backgrounds of us, I realized that I may should first try to adapt to this kind of teamwork and then put forward the suggestion of task plan. So, I felt more integrated afterwaards.
