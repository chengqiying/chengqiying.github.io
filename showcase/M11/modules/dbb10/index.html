Constructive Design Research
Module Introduction

It is a one week module which puts emphasis on design research process. I think I would have a chance to improve my ability of design research through the exercises in this module, which contributes to understanding users as well as the design itself.  The research is divided into three directions as field, lab and showroom. In order to learn professional research approaches in practical context, I chose the topic of field.

With the given theme of Greed and Charity, a research process was conducted by our group to find out the people's different behaviours which could be manifested as greed or charity in interactions with vending machines in society. I really enjoyed th process of this module to work together with the group to search an learn the research methods for field.

Activities

Concept

A classical vending machine has been chosen as the interactive platform for this field study. In this field research an additional device has been added to an existing vending machine in order to test peoples’ reaction and behaviour when offered free additional free candy, after their purchase has been collected. The goal of the field research is to develop knowledge about people’s behaviour and what influences their decisionto take action for taken the additional free candy or not. Various (internal and external) factors can influence people’s behaviour in deciding to take the additional candy or not.

Analysis method

A“Qualitative Data Analysis Methods” were used to interpret and guide the process of the experiment to come to a proper outcome. With the recommendation on the open and closed coding methodsfrom Qualitative field research expert Panos Markopoulos, I learnt  the method together with other fellow members in order to  synthesise a coherent picture from disparate data. We gathered data by video taking, obervation and interview afterwards. In data analysis, all information is summarized into annotated chunks. These chunks are then coded into categories of frequent observations. Recurring themes, rules and relationships are labeled as memos. These aspects describe explanations and personal interpretations of the events during the experiment, from which we get conclusions of the research question:"What influence people to be greedy?"

Research experiment

The experiment is conducted from a piolet test, and follwoed by three kind of prototypes. The final prototype was placed on the chosen vending machine in the Auditorium to provoke participants to decide whether to take additional
free candy or not. In the process of experiment, I learnt that the most improtant point of field research is to observe participants' behaviors in a natural context. The test has been done by one half of the team in order to do a single blind analysis on the test results.

Reflection

In this module, I was in the group of field research. I chose this direction because I want to learn more about focusing on users in a real context. After this one week, I indeed have learned a lot about how to do a field research. Also, in the final presentation and discussion, I learned from the other two groups: lab and showroom, and had a fair understanding of the ways of research in these two directions.

Field research

As for me, I didn’t have much experience about field research before. And now, I think I know that the core of field research is capturing participants’ natural behavior and interaction with the design in the context. In order to gather qualitative data, video recording, observations and following interviews can be taken. Also the analysis of these behaviors is an important part of the research after data gathering. For the analysis of our field research, we used qualitative data analysis methods (open and closed coding methods) to synthesize a coherent picture from disparate data. Through this method the analysis would go beyond summarizing and onto interpretations, to ensure that conclusions drawn are more reliable and useful.In the process of our field research, I learned that for a research goal, the prototype is not the most crucial part of the research. More focus should be put on the sections of data gathering and analysis.

Showroom and lab research

In the discussion, I got a basic understanding of how to conduct a research in lab, and how to gather and analysis quantitative data from the lab group. In some extent, Lab research is the extension of field research, which can get more quantitative evidence for the factors founded in field research.From the showroom group, I learned that trigger participants to think and discuss about these design in the further.
