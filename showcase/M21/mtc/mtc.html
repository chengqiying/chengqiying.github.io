Introduction

Context: It is a one-week module to explore traces of time, use and interaction in materials and reflected on our emotional responses towards various materials. Our team designed a set of rings which helped to explore the symbolic notion of 'Preventable and Unpreventable Ageing'. This notion was translated into combination of two materials. The first material ages through time (organic material) and second material ages through the interaction with it (durable/ long-lasting material).

Goal: In this module, I would like to develop an awareness of materials, how it unconsciously influence our perception about objects and experience. And then see how it could be used as inspirations in design process. Also I expect to understand user experience from the perspective of materiality and try to apply the new insights into user experience design.

What I have learned

Haptic sense could enhance user experience
I have seen a big difference between optical visuality and haptic sense on the final exhibition, which is mentioned by Laura U.Marks. The audiance got pretty surprised when they touch and feel the objects as it was not as same as what they thought when they use their eyes to perceive the objects. Moreover, it is an obvious phenomenon that people have the unconscious tend to feel the objects by touching. This helped me realised that in user experience design, it is vital to create a chance for users to experience the design through multiple senses because it could help them to have a full experience about it. It is the same case in Apple’s design. Also in digital design, the optical visuality gives people the vision of the design and haptic visuality has a connection between vision and the design.

From abstract notion to concrete object
The exploration of how we could narrow down the abstract notion of contrast between natural ageing objects and non ageing man-made materials to a redesigned object, is a process for me to rethink about the relation between these two opposite aspects. I came to realise the transition and integration between the total opposite characteristics(long lasting and natural ageing), which seems has litte possibility to me to combine them before. For me, the practice of narrowing down to an object design is an interpretation of the abstract notion in a concrete level. In this way, it brings me back to an object again.

Further development

The most struggling part for me in the practice to communicate on an abstract level with students in Nijmegen University, because I have been of focusing on concrete designs as a industrial designer. It is of great necessity to select a precise and professional word for one specific notion. The biggest difference between the Nijmegen students and me is the way of thinking. How they interpreted their insights on our notion provided me a more philosophical angel to reflect on our symbolic notion.

In the teamwork, I put myself in a right place to balance my team members’ two different kinds of ways of expressing and information processing. I would take good use of my ability in a team to reconcile discords and also help make the information clear for both sides.
