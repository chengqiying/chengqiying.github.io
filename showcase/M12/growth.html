GROWTH

Bachelor: The four years' education of industrial design in China have developed my skills in design thinking and  ideal and concept training together with the related fundamental skills of sketch, modeling by rhino, prototyping on arduino and graphic design. I have obtained substantial experience of designing for physical interaction and executing with related technologies, which can be seen in the projects on my portfolio website.

 

M11: In the first semester here in TU/e, I set the vision of service design in the beginning guided by the competency based education system which is a fresh new environment. I was grateful to this, because it not only gave me the choice to study service design and also provided the chance to systematically develop my competencies as a designer on an overall level. Through this half year, I have become more comfortable with the system and gradually connected my culture background within the design and life, and I am still on the way of  finding the most suitable way of studying design here.

Present

As I reflected in the learning activities, my main focused competencies are User Focus & Perspective, Designing Business Process and Research Design Process. The rest competencies of Ideas and Concepts, Social Cultural Awareness, Self-directed and Continuous Learning, Teamwork and Communication and Integrated Technology also have been partly developed together.
