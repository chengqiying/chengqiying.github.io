Past

In last semester, I took a starting step to build the structure of home page of my personal website by learning HTML & CSS.  For a further development, firstly I continued studying web building language together with some knowledge of Java Script to complete the portfolio website. This time, I paid more attention on the web structure design than the web layout. I have been aware that the structure also has strong influence on users because it could led them have a clear understanding of  what the web intends to tell and easily find what they want to look at. This experience taught me the knowledge of user experience of on screen products and inspire my interests in it.

 

Besides, it is also a good time to rethink about my vision and identity, which are vital to convey in the portfolio. As it can be reflected from the projects presented in my portfolio, I have spent lots of efforts and interests in service design and interaction design both in my bachelor and master. My interest is still growing in service design with on screen products.
