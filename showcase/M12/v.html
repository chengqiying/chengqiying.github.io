Module DB115

Vedute = 0200 (44x32x7)

Introduction

It is a one week module focusing primarily on Ideas and Concepts, and also Social Cultural Awareness and Form and Senses. For me, it will be an unusual experience to develop ideas and concepts for a spatial manuscript and materialize them into a presentable prototype. I wish in this module, I could take use of the chance to broaden my mind of idea and concepts.

 

This Vedute intends to express the random creativity by transferring the graphic artwork to a spacial one through the perspective structure. Layers of paintings which are supposed to be done by different people would be inserted in the 3D dimensional frame, so in the front side it turns out to be one completed drawing, and in the up side it shows the layers. People could engage in composing one complete drawing by firstly finishing his or her layer of drawing and then integrating different layers together. The underlying value is about connecting people in a composing activity by this Vedute. In this sense, every layer represents one single person, the combined drawing is formed as the society which is constitutes by a variety of people.

 

What I have learned

Elastic Design Thinking

Implementation and crafting

Starting with a brainstorm let me go beyond the regular design thinking focus, for instance the user experience and target function, I could release my mind and explore the interesting ideas which is likely to be implemented in this restrict space. I would see this kind of transition of design thinking as the practice of forming the elastic design thinking, which is surely important for me to broaden my eyes in design process as an industrial designer who intends to offer innovative solutions for people’s needs. In addition, it is the first time I tried to design a spacial manuscript, which is a total new experience based on the restrict space. The connection of freedom and limitation is an interesting point to think about, which reminds me of the moments when designers have to create the innovative ideas, and at the same time have to balance the demands from different aspects. In the process of this module, I could get inspiration for further study and work.

This module is pretty a chance to explore the ability of implementing the ideas into a presentable prototype with a spacial restriction in a short period of time. This process made me again realize the obvious difference between the initial idea in my mind and the physical prototype which are supposed to be made by hands in the end. The shifting between the initial concept and the final result is inevitable in the design process, and it is a process of trying and adjusting. For designers, a complete work is definitely generated by fine tuning in every step of the process.

 In a another respect, the high crafting requirements of the “Vedute”  is really good training for me to make the design object as elegant as possible, which is also considered as one aspect of the design ability for a designer.

Reflection and Future development

It is an effective way to prevent overwhelming people on the

platform. The concept is rigid in a way passengers are not offered with choices, more like following instructions. Behaviour analysis might also be use not only in searching for design opportunities but also on how to implement the persuasions in the system.

 

 

In this module, I have a lot of time to explore various ideas for the special space, and then make it out by a few times of attempt. Meanwhile, I paid effort to explore the underlying value of the design concept. Therefore, in summary I have developed my skill of ideas and concept, form and sense, and also the social and culture awareness.
