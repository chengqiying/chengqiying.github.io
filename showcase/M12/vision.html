In my vision, design promotes comfort and elegance. My intention in design is to help people live more easily and comfortably. Nowadays, the overwhelming numbers of new things (e.g. technologies, new media and systems) have filled people’s lives. What I am interested in is how to assist people to adjust to these changes and challenges through design. In the new era of industrial design, focus is transferring from product itself to user’s experience. A design concept is not only an elegant form and a fantastic function, but a realistic solution which can well address people’s needs and concerns in a specific context.

 

As an emerging discipline in industrial design, service design is attractive to me because its combination of interdisciplinary creativity, design and business. It belongs to design but also goes beyond design. The service design thinking requires to form a holistic map in mind as it is an evolving and complex approach. I believe the exploration of the new language of service design would be a pleasant journey.

As a designer, I want to focus on creating designs which are more concerned about processes and systems. I would see myself as both a designer and a consultant of normal people’ life, the mission of whom is to provide innovative and helpful advice, in order to settle the complaints and dissatisfactions about currently organized services. The design process includes enhancing customer experience and improving business performance across a wide range of industries and sectors. Therefore, it requires to develop the ability of identifying and resolving the needs of different stakeholders at the same time, which is vital in the real design cases.
