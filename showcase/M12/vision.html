GROWTH

In my vision, design promotes comfort and elegance. My intention in design is to help people live more easily and comfortably. Nowadays, the overwhelming numbers of new things (e.g. technologies, new media and systems) have filled people’s lives. What I am interested in is how to assist people to adjust to these changes and challenges through service design. As an emerging discipline in industrial design, service design is attractive to me because its combination of interdisciplinary creativity, design and business. It belongs to design but also goes beyond design. The service design thinking requires to form a holistic map in mind as it is an evolving and complex approach.

 

For M11, I treated it as a exploratory start of service design and I found service design mainly relies on the core of user experience.  In the new era of industrial design, focus is transferring from product itself to user’s experience. A design concept is not only an elegant form and a fantastic function, but a realistic solution which can well address people’s needs and concerns in a specific context. Therefore, in this semester, I still be on the big direction of service design, but more specifically focus on the user interaction experience .

 

When kick-starting the study here last semester, I set a vision of service design although with little experience of it because I am most interested in it and hope to be competent in this area in the future. Defining my personal vision on design to guide the study activities is a fresh new way for me. In some extent, it forces me to decide a specific direction in design and distinguish myself from the rest of fellow students. This previous vision opened a door to service design, which directed me to experience what service design is through my M11 project "Service 2.0".

 

As a designer, I put focus on creating services which are more concerned about processes and systems. In order to become a qualified service designer, an ability of observing daily life and finding out complaints and dissatisfactions about currently organized services is vital at the beginning of this design journey. The design process includes enhancing customer experience and improving business performance across a wide range of industries and sectors.

As a designer, I still want to focus on creating designs which are more concerned about the realistic needs of people. I would see myself as both a designer and a consultant of normal people’ life, the mission of whom is to provide innovative and helpful advice, in order to settle the complaints and dissatisfactions about currently organized services. The design process includes enhancing customer experience and improving business performance across a wide range of industries and sectors. Therefore, it requires to develop the ability of identifying and resolving the needs of different stakeholders at the same time, which is vital in the real design cases.

 

The new gained experience of designing digital interaction triggers my interest. I would continue the journey of exploration of on screen products and forward to the role of UX designer in service design.
