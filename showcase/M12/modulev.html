Module DB115

Vedute = 0200 (44 x 32 x 7)

Introduction

Goals: It is a one week module focusing primarily on Ideas and Concepts, and also Social Cultural Awareness and Form and Senses. For me, it will be an unusual experience to develop ideas and concepts for a spatial manuscript and materialize them into a presentable prototype. I wish in this module, I could take use of the chance to broaden my mind of idea and concepts by experiencing a new type of design without the my typical mind set of designing for users.

 

Activities: My Vedute intends to express the random creativity by transferring the graphic artwork to a spacial one through the perspective structure in a constrained space (44x32x7 cm). Layers of paintings which are supposed to be done by different people would be inserted in the 3D dimensional frame, so in the front side it turns out to be one completed drawing, and in the up side it shows the layers. People could engage in composing one complete drawing by firstly finishing their own layers of drawing and then integrating them together. The underlying value is about connecting people in a composing activity by this Vedute. In this sense, every layer represents one particular person, the combined drawing is formed as the society which is constitutes by a variety of people.

 

What I have learned

Elastic Design Thinking

Implementation and crafting

Starting with a free brainstorm let me go beyond the regular design thinking focus, for instance the user experience and target function, I could release my mind and explore the interesting ideas which is likely to be implemented in this restrict space. I would see this kind of transition of design thinking as the practice of forming the elastic design thinking, which is surely important for me to broaden my eyes in design process as an industrial designer who intends to offer innovative solutions for people’s different needs.  The connection of freedom and limitation is an interesting point to think about, which reminds me of the moments when designers have to create the innovative ideas, but at the same time have to balance the demands from different aspects(clients, market, etc). In the process of this module, I could get inspiration for further study and work.

For me, this module is pretty a chance to explore the ability of implementing the ideas into a presentable prototype with a spacial restriction in a short period of time. This process made me again realize the obvious difference between the initial idea in my mind and the physical prototype which are supposed to be made by hands in the end. The shifting between the initial concept and the final result is inevitable in the design process. It is a process of trying and adjusting. My three iterations  of designing this Vedute showed me that  a complete work is definitely generated by fine tuning in every step of the process. In another respect, the high crafting requirements of  “Vedute”  is really a good training for me to make the design object as elegant and perfect as possible, which is also counted as one aspect of design ability for a designer.

Reflection and Future development

My Vedute turned out to be an elegant and conceptually convincing manuscript. I had a sufficient exploration in materials use and manufacturing techniques. Since I included the users in my Vedute, it would have been much better if I had the opportunity to use the manuscript with several test users. Then a validation could have been made to improve the concept and It would've greatly enhanced the overall exploration. On a social level, I should think about how and when the manuscript is going to be used, and how it possibly relates to the ritual of making something collectively. Later in my project, I saw its potential as an enabling tool to trigger communication between residents and it enlightened me on how to connect people together. When evaluating the idea and concept, there is still a need to optimize it to have a wider narrative and meaning.

 

Overall, I had a lot of time to explore various ideas for the special space, and then made it out by a few times of attempts. Meanwhile, I also paid efforts to explore the underlying value of the design concept. Therefore, in summary I have developed my skill of ideas and concept, form and sense, and also the social and culture awareness.
