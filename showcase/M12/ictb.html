Module DB516

Innovative Concepts to Business

Introduction

This two-week module mainly focused on exploring an attractive way to sell the innovative concepts integrated with business plan to investors. During the process, I expected to learn more professional skills about how to combine design concepts with business and financial in the early stage of concept development, and how to think from the perspective of the investors in order to make the concept  more convincing and realistic.

 

All the four presentations of showing the concept formed into a growth for me and the other members to be passionate and confident about our product. We tried out various tools to analyze the business case, e.g. business model canvas, cash flow and revenue. In the end, we could not only show an appealing concept, but also the market and financial behind to communicate the business opportunity in nowadays market of our concept.

 

What I have learned

Skill of selling the concept to investors

Business issue included in concept

This two-week module teaches me the importance of choosing an effective way to present the wonderful ideas to investors in person. As I can conclude from these two weeks, the first thing is to make the concept clear and appealing to the audience. Because only when the investors could fully understand and be attracted by the concept, then they might have interests to know the rest of the business plan.

According to the feedback from the investors, I learned that, from the perspective of business, not only the concept itself matters, but also the passion of the whole team who are standing there and presenting the concept matters. In the same time when designers are showing the concepts, they are delivering the attitude and temperament of themselves.

It is a great chance for me to think about the business issues (e.g. market, financial) in the concept developing stage. Compared with the previous concept generating process, taking the “how” question into consideration brought me back to reality and make the concept more operational. I also found out that the business angle empowered us to be clearer about the concept development plan in next few years. It helps me to go further for the concept and integrated it with real business instead of staying at the stage of pure design.

Besides, as we are required to figure out the financial part of the concept in the second week, by this time, I obtained the knowledge about the main elements (like cost, market size, revenue and cash flow etc.) in calculating the financial, which are totally fresh to me.

Multi-disciplinary teamwork

In our team, the team members have different background, such as engineering, financial and design. It is pretty good time that we fully make use of our own strengths and work well with each other learn from the others. Although sometime we indeed had a few different points of view, but we communicated and divided the task efficiently, so that our team could improve the concept as well as the presentation based on the feedback. During this process, I think I have trained my skill of cooperating with people from multiple backgrounds as a team and deliver a complete solution.

Through the teamwork and presentations to investor, I really got the experience of how to communicate design concepts with people those have no or little design background. Concerning the different backgrounds, the point is to clearly express by appropriate means which make the concept easy to be understood.

Reflection and Future development

I am really glad with the fluent teamwork in the process and my practice and growth. Still, as the feedback we got from investors in the final presentation said, the motivation of the concept was not attractive enough, we should give more clear and stronger reasons to users why they need our "eboo" digital bookshelf. Because it is pretty important for investors and users to see the problems and demands.  Also, practicing ore innovative and various ways to present the idea could help it to be clearly understood. Although it only could be applied as an application on TV with current technology, we should show the envisioned potentials in the future.

 

In summary, through the learning of integrating the innovative design concept with business issue, I have improved my competencies in Designing Business Processes, User Focus and Perspective, and Self-directed and Continuous Learning.
