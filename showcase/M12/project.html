How can digital service contribute to the sense of community for people living in the same neighborhood ?

Introduction

My project “City Vitality“ is about making concrete neighborhoods more ‘vital’ by identifying and connecting needs of different groups together, and designing from a systems design perspective in this context. The aim of the project is to find out how design can contribute to the sense of community for people living in the same physical neighborhood. “Research Through Design“ framework is adopted as the scheme to guide the research process. The design concept of a community service application is proposed as the main intervention to collect qualitative and quantitative data about residents’ sense of community.

 

The sense of community in Hertoghof was at a quite low level according to the context exploration. It indicated that improvements needed be made in this apartment.  Although the physical environment limits the connection, digital service still has opportunities to be applied and further help residents to build a stronger community.

 

What I have learned

Learning difference between design and research

How to conduct a design research

In the beginning, I tried a lot of ways to figure out the basic structure of research, for example reading the book "Research Methodology" for beginners and consulted an expert  in research Stephan Wensveen for the evaluation method. By studying and trying, I got to understand the biggest difference between design and research.  For design, the final result will come into one kind of design. And for search, the aim is to generate knowledge in the end. Design plays the role of a mean to collect data in evaluation.

The most  important aspect that I have learned in this project is how to conduct and proceed a research. As it can be seen from the process, from finding the research direction to the final analyzing, a lot of new learned methods were used to proceed the research, like pre-test & post-test and co-constructing stories. Looking back to the start, I am very glad about my growth through the process, especially completing the evaluation part in a real context. As a beginner in research, I started with learning the research process by reading related books. By going through this short-term research, I have experienced the several basic steps and  also tried one of the research frameworks "Research through design".

Designing digital product as a solution

Methods of user experience study

Based on the insights from the case study of nowadays design for communities,  I found the potential opportunity of digital product as the  increasing use of smart devices in daily life, like tablet and even smart watch. Hence, integrating the trend and the context, I tried to design a community social application for the residents in Hertoghof. In this process, I used mind mapping to synthesis all the insights gained from the previous exploration part.  In order to have an interactive prototype, I learned to use proto.io to design the interface and interaction for this application. Through this experience, I felt that digital products design could influence the users and help to improve the quality as same as the physical products.

In order to get deep understanding of residents’ sense of community and collect the high quality data for analyzing, I practiced my skills in interviewing users, participatory observation, inviting people to participate in the test, communicating the story to users and adjusting the methods according to different cases. As I invited the residents by knocking the door randomly, so the participants are unpredictable. In this case, I need to flexibly reply to the extreme situations. For example, I encountered a resident who does not use smart phone, so I shifted the prepared questions and method. By this first time to get in depth feedback from real users, I enriched the way to study user experience.

Reflection and Future development

For me, it is a fresh new semester and also a most challenging one, because I need to conduct a research project individually. As I trained myself as a designer in the past so I have no experience in research before.  I started to transfer my mind set from design to design research. Through this project, I realized that research is  a way of thinking and finding answers to the question more than a set of skills. The most exciting aspect for me  is the integration of user experience design and research. I have obtained the experience of testing with the tool of my design in a real user context.

 

 I would take this research project as the first step for a formal research  considering the time, design development and participants, etc., in this semester. Although, in some parts of the process, there are still a few steps need to be improved. Because from the book and my own process, I learned that the reliability of the result mostly relies on the quality of collected data. First of all, in the evaluation method,  I will try out other methods to design the experiment except co-constructing stories, and have an comparison between them, to see the different effects.  Secondly, in the nest round of research, the development of this community application  should go further for real use before evaluation. Thirdly, when analyzing the data, it is important to choose a more professional way in order to let the results make more sense.
