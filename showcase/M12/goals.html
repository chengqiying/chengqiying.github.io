Part 1: Competency of learning

Integrating Technology

I have been aware that the skill of making videos for the expression of design concept and design process is becoming indispensable. I would add it into my learning schedule to enhance my level of video making through practice. In the meantime, I will keep on designing my personal website based on HTMl and CSS. Additionally, I plan to try another way - Adobe Muse for the showcase design.

Self-Directed & Continuous Learning

As independent thinking and learning are very important in the growth of a designer, I am eager to improve my self-directed & continuous learning capability. The crucial point is to plan a reasonable schedule for self–directed time to ensure the study efficiency (e.g. the self-directed learning weeks).

Teamwork & Communication

Having a few experience of teamwork with students from different cultures in the past semester, for me, it is still a long way to go for a more efficient cooperation and better leveraging of my own ability in a team. Communication skills and the English level are two key points I plan to improve through practicing talking to English speakers, reading books (e.g. “This Is Service Design Thinking”) and articles especially for the written English. Besides, I will try to get feedback from coaches with different expertise, which can provide me with various angles to consummate research process and also improve my design.

Part 2: Competency of service design

User Focus & Perspective

I have learned that the user is the core of a design product, system or service as designed things are created to serve people. In the projects I have done in my Bachelor’s, there were actually some user researches contained in every project (for instance, interview, observation, scenarios and role playing), but I think the depth is not enough. Actually, I really put emphases on this competency in the M11 project and modules, consequently I have accumulated much experience of it. To further a sound design research process, I should continue to rich my ways of user research and conduct this part in a more professional way, and also get more useful insights after analyzing.

Designing Business Process

As I know, designed things need to be connected with a business process for being exposed to the market. When I evaluate it now from my past learning, what was lacking is that the business development is not based on enough real data and information which led to defects in the business process. And in service design, understanding the business process of clients is the priority action. I expect to grasp more knowledge about the bridge between concepts and market through the module DB516 Innovative Concepts to Business as well as reading related books.

PDP

First of all, for conducting a research project, I need to start with reading articles about research for beginners. I also expect to grasp the fundamental knowledge of research methods to facilitate the design process. Secondly, user experience is always the core of service design, so I would continue my efforts on efficiently discovering user’s concerns and influencing their behaviors.
