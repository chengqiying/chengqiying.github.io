Visualize the Rhythm of Music

The elf of music, rhythm, usually can not be visually perceived. This project tried to transform it into flickering light with diverse colors that can be captured by people' eyes. The three lights correspond respectively to three primary colors: red, green and blue. When the lights twinkle with music, color appearing in each lamp is related to timbre and the frequency is decided by rhythm.

Translucent Lampshade

Quadrangled mat and triangle glass are the basic components. Pieces of this two kinds of glass are jointed togehter to form the fianl lampshade.
