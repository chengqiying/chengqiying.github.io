Levo for Vogel's

product service
teamwork
duration: 11.2013 (1 week)

Inroduction

This project aims at supporting elderly people to live carefree, independently and autonomously, so that they are confident to function in everyday life. Levo is designed as the mechanical systems that improves the ergonomics of the kitchen, and enhance the accessibility of cooking utensils and appliances.

Concept

Levo installed in topshelves and the bottom drawer.

Field and Trend Research

Target group: the number of older adults is increasing. Older adults want to live in their own home as long as possible. The growth of our current health care system does not suffice the needed care for this growing population. Therefore smart solutions in houses need to enable this autonomy and provide care instead.
needs in kitchen: people increasingly value the quality of food, cooking and dining.The kitchen changes from a closed space to a part of the house where you invite others for this important social activity.
Opportunity: combining our design values, Vogel’s competencies, societal changes and trends within the target group, we believe that Vogel’s can expand the kitchen experience.

Business Model

Vogel's: vogel’s already has engineering expertise and knowledge to develop the product. Production can be outsourced to their existing partner and new product will be sold through retail partners.
IKEA: IKEA can introduce this new product line to an existing market. A deal will be made so IKEA has exclusive rights for the first years, so Vogel’s builds brand confidence in this new market.
Future plan: in the future, Vogel’s can start collaborating with for example Bruynzeel and create “Vogel’s kitchens” integrated the mechanisms. At this stage, more revenue streams can be created.

Customer Journey

Customer: our target group searches for a new kitchen solution because the current one is becomming inaccessible due to physical restraints.
Brand: we believe buyers of the LEVO system will become embassadors of the Vogel’s brand. When friends visit the kitchen of our customers, they would be impressed by the comfort and ease.
Retail shopping: many older adults will go to a retail shop either for face-to-face advice. It is important for Vogel’s to introduce them to the high-margnin products that they can sell.
Vogel's market: vogel’s can market their product at the moment a customer starts browsing for a whole new kitchen or for a new solution for his current kitchen, both online and real stores.
