City Vitality

Service Research

Individual

Duration: 9 weeks

02.2014 - 06.2014

Inroduction

The project “City Vitality“ is about making concrete neighborhoods more ‘vital’ by identifying and connecting needs of different groups together, and designing from a systems design perspective in this context. The aim of the project is to find out how design can contribute to the sense of community for people living in the same physical neighborhood. “Research Through Design“ framework is adopted as the scheme to guide the research process. The design concept of a community service application is proposed as the main intervention to collect qualitative and quantitative data about residents’ sense of community.

Approach

Context Exploration

Various insights from four kinds of context exploration methods were showing the same situation that the sense of community in Hertoghof was at a quite low level. In the other hand, it indicated that improvements needed be made in this apartment. Although the physical environment limits the connection, digital service still has opportunities to be applied and further help residents to build a stronger community.

Ideation and Prototype

Having a basic understanding of the situation of residents’ sense of community and the analysised reasons from the context exploration session, therefore, the possible concepts can focus on enhacing the three lack aspects of sense of community through building the fundamental connection between residents of Hertoghof. The first step is to provide the chance for them to know their neighbors around, e.g who are they and where do they com from? The futher step is to facilitate them to start a conversation. The ideas are intergrated into one ios social application prototype.

1. Post

Users can post statuses on the regular timeline, the name and room number are shown aside the portrait.

2. Comment

On the location-based interface, users can add comments to the posted statuses and they will appear on the mapped place.

3. Check location

Users get the opportunity to know the neighbors living around by the room map. All these private information is provided by the users themself voluntarily.

Evaluation

The evaluation session intended to collect a pool of quantitative and qualitive data. The qantitative part consists of the average scores in four aspects of sense of commnunity in both pre-test and post-test. For the qualitative part, it contains the residents’ feedback of the design concept of the communication application and the reasons related to the quantitative scores.

Result

In summary, the statistic charts of experiment group C show the overall result that the average level of four aspects of the sense of community in Hertoghof has a rise from pre-test to posttest, with the intervention of the design of the community application. Therefore, it can be concluded that the digital community application could have possible contribution to the rise of the sense of community in Hertoghof, based on the analyzed reasons of the increase.
