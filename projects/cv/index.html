City Vitality

digital service
individual
duration: 02.2014 - 06.2014 (9 weeks)

Inroduction

“City Vitality“ aims to find out how design can contribute to the sense of community for people living in the same physical neighborhood. “Research Through Design“ framework is adopted to guide the research process. The design concept of a community service application is proposed as the main intervention to collect qualitative and quantitative data about residents’ sense of community.

Prototype

Post: users can post statuses on the regular timeline, the name and room number are shown aside the portrait.
Comment: on the location-based interface, users will appear on the mapped place after they add comments to the posted statuses.
Check location: users get the opportunity to know the neighbors living around by the room map. All these private information is provided by the users themself voluntarily.

Approach

Context Exploration

Various insights from four kinds of context exploration methods were showing the same situation that the sense of community in Hertoghof was at a quite low level. Although the physical environment limits the connection, digital service still has opportunities to be applied and further help residents to build a stronger community.

Evaluation

The evaluation session intended to collect a pool of quantitative and qualitive data. The qantitative part consists of the average scores in four aspects of sense of commnunity in both pre-test and post-test. IN the qualitative part, it contains the residents’ feedback of the design concept of the communication application.

Result

The statistic charts of group C show the average level of the sense of community in Hertoghof has a rise in post-test, with the intervention of the design of the community application. It can be concluded that the digital community application could have possible contribution to the rise of the sense of community in Hertoghof, based on the analyzed reasons of the increase.
