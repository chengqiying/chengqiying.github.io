All Sections processed in the Cloud

The complete service design procedure is contained in the cloud. Farmers just need to upload the information about the produce to the resource pool. Based on these, the design ecosystem will create the sound products for consumer.

Procedure

The Service

1. Uploading and Evaluation

Farmers upload the basic informaiton of the produce into the resource pool in the cloud and the selected will get evaluations from assessors afterwards.

2. Investment

Investors have the access to the evaluations and investing money in the most potential produce.

3. Planning and Marketing

Food designers will work on those produce that get the investment. Marketers cooperate with designers to bring this products into the market.

4. Logistics and Consume

Finally, the complete products are transported to the retail for consuming
