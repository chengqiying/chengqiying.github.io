Get Information by A Simple and Habitual Action

Information wall simplifies the approaches to get information by connecting the daily physical objects with the accompanying information and presenting the information in the place where the objects usually be. In this case, the information wall becomes an example of the home environment in this project to show the information of the objects which would be placed or hung on the wall.

Decide the Information Carried by Objects

The six objects: umbrella, wallet, keychain, calendar, music-tap and book, were selected based on the places where they are usually supposed to be. They are daily used and often placed on or around the wall at home. But the information accompanied with the objects is received by people through complex means. The close relationship between the information and the objects were widely explored when identifying the information.
