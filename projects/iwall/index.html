Inforamtion Wall

Interaction Design

Individual

Duration: 8 weeks

04.2013-05.2013

Get Information by A Simple and Habitual Action

Information wall simplifies the approaches to get information by connecting the daily physical objects with the accompanying information and presenting the information in the place where the objects usually be. In this case, the information wall becomes a carrier of the home environment in this project to show the information of the objects that would be placed or hung on the wall.

Decide the Information Carried by Objects

umbrella--weather forecast

wallet--consumption records

keychain--routes

calendar--schedule

music tape-- music diplay

book--reading-aloud

Select Objects

The six objects: umbrella, wallet, keychain, calendar, music-tap and book, were selected based on the home environment and the places where they are usually supposed to be. The common thing is that they are daily used and often placed on or around the wall at home.

Information

The repsented information is accompanied in the objects and reached by people by more complex means. The close relationship between the information and the objects were widely explored when identifying the information.

Opportunity

Combining our design values, Vogel’s competencies, societal changes and trends within the target group, we believe that Vogel’s can expand the kitchen experience.

Ideation

"A day in Life" was used to analyze the the elderly's rountine on one day of his o her life. By drawing this map, we got inspirations from the different timelines. It could obviously be seen that for elderly people, a lot of the time was spent in the kitchen. Therefor, we got the desin point for Vogel's in the kitchen facilities.

The Product —LEVO

Levo installed in topshelves and the bottom drawer.

Business Model

Vogel's

First of all, Vogel’s already has engineering expertise and knowledge within their company which is needed to develop the product. Secondly, production of the product can be outsourced to their existing partner. Vogel’s new product will be sold through retail partners.

IKEA

IKEA is a good example, they can introduce this new product line to an existing market. A deal will be made so IKEA has exclusive rights for the first years, so Vogel’s builds brand confidence in this new market.

Future Plan

In the future Vogel’s can start collaborating with for example Bruynzeel and create “Vogel’s kitchens”. These kitchens have the mechanisms integrated. At this stage, more revenue streams can be created through other retail stores.

Customer Journey

Customer

Our target group searches for a new kitchen solution because the current one is becomming inaccessible due to physical restraints. They do not have much awareness of the different offers on the market.

Brand

Finally we believe that buyers of the LEVO system will become embassadors of the Vogel’s brand. When friends and familie visit the homes and kitchen of our customers, they should be impressed with the comfort and ease that the LEVO system brings to the owner.

Retail Shopping

After browsing for solutions, many older adults will go to a retail shop either for face-to-face advice (as shown in the user insights) or to purchase the kitchen solution. It is very important for Vogel’s to train the retailers and introduce them to the high-margnin products that they can sell. This can be done in a similar way as with Mediamarket and TV mounting solutions.

Vogel's Market

Vogel’s can market their product at the moment a customer starts browsing for a whole new kitchen or for a new solution for his current kitchen. By using a Kitchenfitter system online and in store (similar to the TV fitter) a customer can easily choose the perfect sollution, tailered to his kitchen,or he can order modular parts for a whole new kitchen.
