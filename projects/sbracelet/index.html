Self-Guided Bracelet

Concept Product Design

Individual

Duration: 2 weeks

10.2011

Design Based on Wireless Technology for Sharing

"Traveling" is a bracelet which can provide aerial views of scenic areas, the traffic flow and preview of a specific spot, and recommended spots around. After the tour, tourists can upload their routes to the cloud storage for sharing.

Ideation

Simple Interaction by Sliding

Switching

The four funtions can be easily switched by finger sliding on the surface of the bracelet.

Zooming

The content is possible t be zoomed in and out for a clear view in different contexts.

Use

There are four main functions in the bracelet. The first one provides a preview of the chosen scenic spot, the second gives the recommendation of the scenic spots around the person, the third one hepls to check the visitor flowrate of a certain spot, and the last function is about recording and uploading tour routes to the cloud storage, and share with others afterwards.
