Foldable Stool

Concept Product Design

Team: Qiying Cheng,

Rong Yu, Shaoting Gu

Duration: 1 week

11.2011

The Chinese Mindset: Yin and Yang

This design comes out of the Chinese traditional mindset of Yin and Yang. The stool is made of a wood board with legs hidden in the body. The stool brings convenience to transportation as it will take much less space than the normal stools.

Save Space

Unfolding

Unfold the hidden legs, the board turns into a seat. When unneeded, it can be folded into a plat board and stacked up.

Bending Structure

The legs are jointed with the body of the stool by a flexiable bending structure.
