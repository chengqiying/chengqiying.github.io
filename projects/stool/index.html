Foldable Stool

concept product desing
teamwork
duration: 11.2011 (1 week)

The Chinese Mindset: Yin and Yang

This design comes out of the Chinese traditional mindset of Yin and Yang. The stool is made of a wood board with legs hidden in the body. The stool brings convenience to transportation as it will take much less space than the normal stools.

Saving Space

Unfolding: Unfold the hidden legs, the board turns into a seat. When unneeded, it can be folded into a plat board and stacked up.
Bending structure: The legs are jointed with the body of the stool by a flexiable bending structure.
