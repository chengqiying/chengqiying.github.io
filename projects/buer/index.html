New Platform of Filmmaking

An increasing number of poeple who love films dream to make films by themselves, however it is not an easy thing for ordanary people. Thus buer is a new platform of filmmaking where ordinary people will have a chance to participate in the process of filmmaking. For example, they can come up with the interseting topics, describe a scene, send a simple video footage ect.

From Fragmentary Footages to A Complete Film

For every film made by buer, there are five main steps of soliciting. The themes, scripts and videos are all selected from those resources users upload on our website. Video cutting and film releasing will be an integrated part, which will be primarily taken by buer studio.
