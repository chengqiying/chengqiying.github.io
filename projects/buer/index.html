New Platform of Filmmaking

A increasing number of poeple dream to make a film, however it is not an easy thing for ordanary people. Thus buer is designed as a new platform of filmmaking. Ordinary people who love films will have a chance to participate in the process of filmmaking. For example, they can come up with the interseting topics, describe a scene, send a simple video footage ect.

From Fragmentary Footages to A Complete Film

For every film made by buer, there are five main steps soliciting: theme soliciting, script soliciting, videos soliciting, cutting and releasing. The first three steps are based on people's engagement as the themes, scripts and videos are all selected from those resources users upload on our website. The rest steps including video cutting and film releasing play as an integrated part, which will be primarily taken by buer studio.
