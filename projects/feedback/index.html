social media feedback system

Social Media
Feedback System

service design
individual
duration: 09.2013 - 12.2013 (9 weeks)

Inroduction

The client of this project is ING (International Netherland Group).Aligned with the needs of customers and the client (ING), this project designs a social media feedback system for ING. The goal is to give customers the impression that their feedback is valued and appreciated by the bank, meanwhile help the bank make efficient use of the feedback from customers. This projects works on a social media platform, and one of the most popular social media, Facebook, is a trial in the project.

Concept

Creat an easier way for customers to give feedback. Design a more efficient way for ING to collect feedback and also make customers interested and feel valued.
Email introduction: In the concept of this stage, I chose email as the main way to introduce the new feedback system to customers. Brochure and advertisements are also included. In the second email, ING would mention the number of the customer’s friends who are using the system on Facebook as a more persuasive introduction.
Biomimicry desgin method: In this section, I tried to visulize the process of feedback generating, being dealt with and decreasing by a kind of living creature. I went through two ways to design the dynamic process. In the first version, the simple colourful circles represent the feedback. The improved one is that the virus’s lifecycle represents the changing process of feedback.
Social media platform: This projects works on a social media platform as it is becoming the popular and essential platform to express and communicate, therefore one of the most popular social media, Facebook is a trial in the project for this semester.

Approach

Context Exploration

Various insights from four kinds of context exploration methods were showing the same situation that the sense of community in Hertoghof was at a quite low level. In the other hand, it indicated that improvements needed be made in this apartment. Although the physical environment limits the connection, digital service still has opportunities to be applied and further help residents to build a stronger community.

Evaluation

The evaluation session intended to collect a pool of quantitative and qualitive data. The qantitative part consists of the average scores in four aspects of sense of commnunity in both pre-test and post-test. For the qualitative part, it contains the residents’ feedback of the design concept of the communication application and the reasons related to the quantitative scores.

Result

In summary, the statistic charts of experiment group C show the overall result that the average level of four aspects of the sense of community in Hertoghof has a rise from pre-test to posttest, with the intervention of the design of the community application. Therefore, it can be concluded that the digital community application could have possible contribution to the rise of the sense of community in Hertoghof, based on the analyzed reasons of the increase.
