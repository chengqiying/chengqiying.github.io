Inroduction

The client of this project is ING (International Netherland Group). This project designs a social media feedback system for ING. The goal is to creat an easier way for customers to give feedback and a more efficient way for ING to collect feedback and also make customers interested and feel valued. This projects works on a social media platform, and one of the most popular social media Facebook as a trial.

Concept

Email introduction: email is the main way to introduce the new feedback system to customers. To be more persuasivethe number of the customer’s friends who are using the system on Facebook. Brochure and advertisements are also included.
Biomimicry desgin method: the virus’s lifecycle represents the changing process of feedback generating, being dealt with and decreasing are presented by a kind of living creature.
Social media platform: this project works on a social media platform as it is becoming the popular and essential platform to express and communicate, therefore one of the most popular social media, Facebook is a trial.

Field Research

In the current feedback system, there are four main ways for the customers to give their feedback to the bank: Facebook message, ING web, email and phone call. ING usually gets a score for their service from customers through an email questionnaire. Then they calculate the average socore every week for evaluation their services and products.

ING's Need: "We need customers’ feedback to improve and deliver a better service for them, and we also want to be close to users and maintain the customer loyalty."
Customer's Need: "We need a more handy way to express our feelings of the services. We expect to know what would happen after we give our feedback to the bank."

Evaluation

Summing up from the user and client evaluation, the holistic concept suits the needs of both customers and the bank. There are still some details could be improved, like the dynamic process after ING has improved the service and the interface for ING staff to check the inital infromation etc. See more feedback.
