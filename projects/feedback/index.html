Social Media Feedback System

In collaboration with ING

Service Design

Duration: 9 weeks

09.2013—12.2013

Introduction

The client of this project is ING (International Netherland Group). ING’s mission is to set the standard in helping its customers manage their financial future. ING aims to deliver its financial products and services in the way its customers want them delivered: with exemplary service, convenience and at competitive prices.

Aligned with the needs of customers and the client (ING), this project designs a social media feedback system for ING. The goal of this project is to give customers the impression that their feedback is valued and appreciated by the bank, meanwhile help the bank make efficient use of the feedback from customers. This projects works on a social media platform, and one of the most popular social media, Facebook, is a trial in the project for this semester.

Field Research

Through the research on ING, it could be concluded that in the current feedback system of ING, there are four main ways for the customers to give their feedback to the bank: Facebook message, ING web, email and phone call. As for the dealing method of these feedback, ING usually gets a score about their service from customers through the email questionnaire. Then they calculate the average socore every week for evaluation their services and products.

ING's Need

"We need customers’ feedback to improve and deliver a better service for them, and we also want to be close to users and maintain the customer loyalty."

Customer's Need

"We need a more handy way to express our feelings of the services. We expect to know what would happen after we give our feedback to the bank."

Service Vision

Creat an easier way for customers to give feedback. Design a more efficient way for ING to collect feedback and also make customers interested and feel valued.

Ideation

In this session, I took two iterations of brainstorm using the storybord to analyze the current feedback system of ING, and a customer journey map for clarificaiton of the painpoints. The general and detailed concepts were generated in this process, then improved and evaluated afterwards.

The Feedback System Service

Email Introduction

In the concept of this stage, I chose email as the main way to introduce the new feedback system to customers. Brochure and advertisements are also included. In the second email, ING would mention the number of the customer’s friends who are using the system on Facebook as a more persuasive introduction.

Biomimicry Desgin Method

TIn this section, I tried to visulize the process of feedback generating, being dealt with and decreasing by a kind of living creature. I went through two ways to design the dynamic process. In the first version, the simple colourful circles represent the feedback. The improved one is that the virus’s lifecycle represents the changing process of feedback

Social Media Platform

This projects works on a social media platform as it is becoming the popular and essential platform to express and communicate, therefore one of the most popular social media, Facebook is a trial in the project for this semester.

User Test and Evaluation

It could be summed up from the user and client test. The holistic concept suited the needs of both customers and the bank. Concerning the advice from users and client,there were still some details could be improved, like the dynamic process after ING have improved the service, the interface for ING staff to check the inital infromation etc. Berfore going to the final cocept, more attention should be paid on this points.

Feedback from ING

—The dynamic process is very attractive and it creats an access for customers to see the process of feedback dealing.
—The growing virus is benificial for the customers to speak their opinions out.
—The idea of social media platform provides customers a more handy way to express.
—The message for informing improved service indeed makes customers feel valued.
—The dynamic process needs to be continued after ING have improved.
—The work to categorize the feedback is still huge for ING staff. It may be better if the customers could sort out the feedback when they post.
—A statistical interface of the feedback would be better for the ING staff to work on the problems

Feedback from Customer

—The idea to visualize the textual feedback isninteresting. It can simplify the quantatitive feedback.
—The vivid dynamic process can bring more customers to participate in the process.
—To introduce the new system by two emails is a persuasive strategy. It can help to convice the customers.
—Replies to customers are also needed after customers post the feedback and when ING starts to put efforts on the problems. This would bring more involvement of customers.

Customer Journey

After thinking about the feedback from users and the client and analyzing the current concept, solutions of the listed lacking details would be worked out in the final concept.
