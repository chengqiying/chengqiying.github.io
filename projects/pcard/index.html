Sprouting Postcard

Concept Product Design

Team: Qiying Cheng,

Shaoting Gu

Duration: 1 week

11.2011

A Postcard with Growth of Plant Life

The stamp of the postcard is transformed into a piece of soil contented with seeds sprout on the way to the destination. When receiving the postcard, receivers also get an extra piece of green grass, which can be taken out of the card and replanted into the flowerpot.

The Scent from Distant Friends

Miss Delivery

Our friends can smell the soil where the postcard sent from by the piece of the grown grass. The design tends to add more fun in sending and receivving postcards.
