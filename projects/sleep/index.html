Subjective Sleep Monitoring

digital service
individual
duration: 10.2014 - 06.2015 (6 months)

Inroduction

With the newly arisen technology, it has become feasible to monitor objective sleep data via various applications. Whereas, data from subjective monitoring is an indispensable branch for a complete picture of sleep state. This project explores digital and interactive solutions which could support people with sleep complaints in monitoring their sleep quality in an unobtrusive manner, and present an useful and lucid visulization.

Concept

Social platform: users will be provided with channels of topics related to sleep and activities. They could post statuses and check in. All the data has been posted will be stored, filtered and presented in user profiles. It aims to bring users a sense of supporting increase the motivation of social sharing and communication on this application.

see prototype

Notification center: all the recoding questions will pop up at different time on the notification center and users have the control of answering it or not. A short animation which would give a review of the sleep quality of that day. The sleep history will be in a calendar with five different colors.

see prototype

Emoticon Expression: emoticons are used to let users choose an overall feeling of their sleep. A personalized timeline will be provided to put the sleep related activities in. Users could see the sleep quality data together with the reasons behind it.

see prototype

User Centered Approach

Empathize users to get insights for concept generating and design decisions. Evaluate designs with users (including clients and experts) and gather feedback for next design loop. These two parts are conducted in different iterations to guide design process.

Design with Users, Experts and Client

The inspirations collected from the participatory design were used to facilitate the ideation process. Three concepts have been generated and developed into prototypes.

To Be Continued

This project is half way finished at this stage. Following, another interation will be conducted to have a final concept and prototype it out for evaluation.
